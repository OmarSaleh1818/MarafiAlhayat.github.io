Services

Your Trusted logistics company in Saudi Arabia

Air Freight

Discover swift and dependable air freight solutions with Marafi Alhayat. Our tailored air freight services ensure punctual deliveries, prioritizing safety and efficiency.

Ocean Freight

Experience smooth and budget-friendly ocean freight solutions with us. We guarantee timely delivery and professional handling of your goods, whether they are small or large, ensuring a hassle-free experience.

Land transport

We guarantee the safe and timely arrival of your shipments. Our reliable and cost-effective land transportation services include road and rail options, offering you peace of mind throughout the journey.

Warehousing

Benefit from our secure and tailored warehousing services designed to meet your unique requirements. We provide cost-effective and flexible storage solutions, ensuring the safekeeping of your goods.

Customs Clearance

Experience seamless import and export processes with us. Our expert customs professionals ensure efficient and smooth customs clearance services for all your international shipments, providing you with peace of mind.

Buyers consolidation

We provide buyers consolidation by combining shipments from multiple customers into a single one to provide efficiency and cost-effectiveness saving your time and resources.
